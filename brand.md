# docfonts - brand (working notes)

Concise positioning to guide copy until a full brand system is generated (run `/brand`). This is not
the full identity - palette, type, logo, and tagline set are TODO.

## What it is
The open, measured registry of substitutes for proprietary document fonts.

## One-liner
Measured open substitutes for proprietary document fonts - with proof, provenance, face coverage, and
a fallback verdict.

## Voice
Neutral, technical, evidence-first. We report measured truth, including negative results. No hype. We
never claim metric-safety without a measurement.

## Claims discipline (load-bearing)
- OK: "measured", "advance delta X%", "no open metric substitute found", "cell-width-only",
  "Built by the team behind SuperDoc".
- NOT OK: "metric-safe" without a measurement; "used by SuperDoc's font resolver" until SuperDoc
  actually consumes the registry; "solves all font issues" (the scope is document-font substitution,
  not web-perf / CLS / CJK / embedding / distribution).

## Audience
Developers and document-engine builders needing document-fidelity font fallback; anyone currently
relying on folklore substitution lists.

## Relationship to SuperDoc
Standalone and neutral. SuperDoc is one consumer and the credibility source, surfaced secondarily.
Never branded as a SuperDoc accessory - the neutrality is what earns the trust that points back.
